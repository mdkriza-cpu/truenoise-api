What does aircraft noise
actually do to the human body?

TrueNoise is an independent community noise monitoring project operating under the departure corridors of Baltimore/Washington International Airport. We use calibrated instruments and published psychoacoustic science to measure what aircraft noise actually does to the human body — not just how loud it is, but how the body responds to each event.

Independent. Transparent. Community-driven.

TrueNoise is not affiliated with BWI Airport, the FAA, or any airline or operator. Every observation is made by community members using calibrated equipment. Every health claim is sourced to peer-reviewed research. All raw data is freely available for download.
